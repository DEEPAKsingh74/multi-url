Multi-URL Opener Help

How to use:

  • Enter multiple URLs in the text area (one per line / space separated)
  • Click "Open All URLs" to open them in new tabs
  • You can Select complete document, then right click and click on Save selected URL in the option menu
  • View saved URLs in the "Saved URLs" tab

Tips:

  • Saved URLs persist between browser sessions
  • Click the trash icon to remove individual saved URLs
  • Click on Clear saved URLs to remove all saved URLs at once.
